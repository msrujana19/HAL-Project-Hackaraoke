Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to track my period?

Tracking your period is easy! Use our app to log your cycle dates and receive reminders for tracking your flow, symptoms, and moods.

How can I improve my water intake?

Try to drink at least 8 cups (2 liters) of water daily. Set reminders on your phone or our app to stay hydrated throughout the day.

How can I manage stress during menopause?

Regular exercise, yoga, deep breathing, and speaking to a healthcare provider for support can help manage stress during menopause.

What are the benefits of tracking my symptoms?

Tracking your symptoms can help identify patterns, manage your health better, and communicate effectively with your healthcare provider.

How can I deal with postpartum depression?

Seeking support from a therapist, reaching out to loved ones, and practicing self-care are key steps. Our community and resources are here to help you.
